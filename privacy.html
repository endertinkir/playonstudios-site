Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-21

This Privacy Policy explains how Play On Studios ("Play On Studios", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile games, including Zen Mahjong, and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Device and technical information, such as device model, operating system, app version, language, country, IP address, and app or advertising identifiers.
  • Gameplay and analytics information, such as session activity, level starts and completions, feature usage, in-game events, and general interaction data.
  • Advertising and consent information, such as ad impressions, clicks, attribution data, advertising identifiers (where available), consent choices, and privacy settings selections.
  • Purchase information, such as product identifiers, transaction identifiers, purchase status, restore events, and entitlement status for in-app purchases or subscriptions.
  • Information you choose to provide if you contact us for support.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our games and services.
  • To analyze gameplay, usage, and performance.
  • To deliver, measure, and optimize advertising.
  • To process purchases and restore entitlements.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Advertising, Analytics, and Consent

We use third-party analytics and advertising technologies, including Google Firebase Analytics, Google AdMob, Meta/Facebook, RevenueCat, and advertising mediation or demand partners that may include Unity Ads, Vungle, and Meta Audience Network.

Where required by law, we request user consent before using certain analytics or advertising features. Users in supported regions may be able to manage ad-related privacy choices through the in-app Privacy Settings screen and/or device settings.

On iOS, we may request permission under Apple's AppTrackingTransparency framework before accessing the advertising identifier for tracking or advertising measurement.

If you consent to ad-related data use, Google and our advertising partners may use advertising identifiers, app activity, and related signals for ad measurement, attribution, remarketing, personalization, bidding, optimization, audience creation, and conversion measurement. Google may also associate consented activity with information from users signed in to Google, subject to Google's own policies and user settings. Users can learn more about Google's controls, including My Activity, at https://myactivity.google.com/.

Our games may use third-party SDKs and services, including:

These third parties may collect or receive information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

How We Share Information

  • With service providers, analytics providers, advertising partners, attribution providers, and payment or subscription providers that help us operate the game.
  • When required by law or when necessary to protect our rights, users, or services.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the game, provide support, process purchases, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal information. To submit a privacy request or ask questions about this policy, contact us at hello@playonstudios.co.

Our games are not directed to children under 13, or under a higher minimum age where required by local law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to This Policy
